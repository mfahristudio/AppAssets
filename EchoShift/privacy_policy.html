Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 8, 2026

M.Fahri Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the mobile game Echo Shift. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our players, including children. This document outlines how data is handled in Echo Shift.

1. Children's Privacy (Families Policy & COPPA)

Echo Shift is designed for players of all ages, including children under the age of 13. We take children's privacy extremely seriously. In compliance with the Child Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play Families Policies:

  • We do NOT collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) from children.
  • No registration or accounts are required to play the game.
  • All third-party advertising requests are strictly flagged as child-directed.

2. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

To keep the game free to play, we serve ads using the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob). AdMob may collect and share certain device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID, IP addresses, or device specifications) to deliver advertising, prevent fraud, and analyze ad performance.

Because our game is targeted towards families, we configure AdMob to:

  • Disable personalized/interest-based tracking for all ad requests.
  • Enforce a Maximum Ad Content Rating of G (General), ensuring only child-safe ads are displayed.

You can learn more about how Google handles ad data in their privacy policy: Google Privacy & Terms.

3. European Area Users (GDPR Compliance)

For players located in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom, we integrate the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK. This consent management system allows users to explicitly review and choose their consent parameters for ad delivery before any ads are initialized. If consent is declined, no personalized tracking data will be shared.

4. Data Security

Since the game operates local offline gameplay loops and high scores are saved locally on your device storage, we do not transmit any personal game stats to external servers.
